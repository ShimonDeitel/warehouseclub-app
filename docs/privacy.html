Privacy Policy

Warehouseclub is an offline-first application. All data you enter (log membership fee plus savings per trip) is stored locally on your device only, in your device's application storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics services, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party trackers embedded in this app.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We never receive or store your payment information.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed with it. We have no servers and no way to access your data remotely.
